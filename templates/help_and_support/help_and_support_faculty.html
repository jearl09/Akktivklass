Getting Started Guide

  • After registering, verify your email and log in. Complete your profile for a personalized experience.
  • Use the dashboard's "Create New Class" button, fill in the details, and invite students.
  • Add, remove, or group students for better class organization. Use the import feature for bulk actions.

Best Practices

  • Use consistent naming, archive old classes, and utilize groups for differentiation.
  • Take attendance regularly, use notes for absences, and review patterns weekly.
  • Use strong passwords, enable two-factor authentication, and never share your credentials.
